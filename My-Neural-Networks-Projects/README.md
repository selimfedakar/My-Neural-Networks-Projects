# AI Development Log: Building Neural Networks

My intensive journey into the mathematical foundations of AI. As a Software Engineering
student who moved from Washington, D.C. to Los Angeles, I am deep-diving into the core of
**Neural Networks** and, in a companion repository, **LLM foundations (Stanford CS336)**.

Every day is one folder with the same three parts: my **handwritten notes**, a **README**
explaining what I learned in my own words, and a small **practice implementation** I wrote
to make the concept concrete. I learn it by building it.
